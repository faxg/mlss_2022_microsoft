### How to get free certification vouchers?

- **for Students**

  - see the [Student Hub](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/learn/student-hub/)
  - Go to the [Cloud Ready Skills] portal (https://msftstudentcert.cloudreadyskills.com/?azure-portal=true)
  - If available in your region / time: [EU Skill Forward](https://itm.cloudreadyskills.com/landing/europe)

- **for University instructors (professors, teachers, assistants etc)**
  - See [Microsoft Learn for Educators](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/learn/educator-center/programs/msle/overview) program.
  - Register as individual teachers or as "instituation"
  - As an MSLE institution, your school receives nice benefits, such as free cert vouchers, lab seats etc.

**Note**: Only some certifications may be available for "free". If in doubt or can't find what you are looking for,
